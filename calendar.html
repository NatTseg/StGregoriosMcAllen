Schedule

Services & Feasts

The Holy Qurbana is normally served on the 2nd and 4th Sundays of each month, with Morning Prayer beforehand. Major feasts may fall on other days — please check below or contact the parish to confirm.

Sunday Worship

7:30 AM — Morning Prayer
8:00 AM — Holy Qurbana
Fellowship meal to follow.

Confession

By appointment with the priest, before the Qurbana or during the week. Please reach out ahead of time.

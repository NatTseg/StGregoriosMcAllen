Welcome

An ancient faith, a small family

Our community traces its worship to the Apostle Thomas, who according to tradition brought the Gospel to India in AD 52. We gather to celebrate the Holy Qurbana in the West Syriac rite — in Malayalam, Syriac, and English — and to welcome anyone seeking the living tradition of the Orthodox Church.

What to expect

Worshiping with us

The Holy Qurbana

Our central act of worship is the Holy Qurbana — the Holy Eucharist of the Syriac Orthodox tradition, served according to the Liturgy of St. James.

Everyone is welcome

Visitors of every background are welcome. You don’t need to know the prayers or the language — come, sit, and pray with us.

After the service

We share a simple meal and fellowship together after the Qurbana. Stay, ask questions, and get to know the parish.
